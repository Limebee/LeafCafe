Our Story

About Us

Leaf Cafe brings organic baked goods and coffee to our customers. We believe in providing food made fresh with locally-sourced ingrdients. Our mission is to provide quality food that contributes to a better environment.

Our Cafe provides a variety of options that accommodates different dietary needs. We provide vegan and gluten-free pastries. Also, we provide non-dairy milk options for coffee. Our goal is to make sure every customer can enjoy our food.

Our Owner - Shelley McAdams

Shelley has worked in the culinary industry for over 15 years. She trained as a pastry chef in Boston. Shelley is a Maplewood native and decided to combine her love of pastries and coffee. She noticed a lack of cafes that offered organic food in the area. Wanting to make a difference, she decided to open Leaf Cafe.
